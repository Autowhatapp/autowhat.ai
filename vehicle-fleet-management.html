Key Platform Capabilities

Real-Time Command & Control

Gain 24/7 visibility into your entire fleet with interactive analytics, 3D visualization, and a centralized call desk module for instant breakdown and ticket management.

Digital Gate & Dock Operations

Fully digitize the vehicle entry lifecycle, including dock assignments, loading checklists, and departure clearances. Our offline-first web architecture ensures field officers can queue data and capture images even during network outages.

Predictive TAT & Multi-Source Tracking

Eliminate tracking blindspots by fusing data from FastTag RFID, SIM triangulation, and direct transporter APIs. Our intelligent engine automatically computes Turnaround Time (TAT) compliance, predicts delays, and alerts you to delivery failures.

Automated Security & KYC

Secure field operations with passwordless WhatsApp OTP authentication. Automatically validate vehicle RCs, fitness certificates, and driver licenses at the gate via integration with government VAHAN and Sarathi APIs.

Vendor Collaboration & Sustainability

Streamline operations with a dedicated self-service portal for transporters to upload Proof of Delivery (POD) documents. Track per-shipment CO2 emissions to easily monitor and achieve your sustainability goals.
