Why Manufacturing Units Choose AutoGate

Intelligent Dispatch Optimization

Transform messy Excel-based planning into a structured, automated workflow. Our Vehicle Assignment Engine automatically groups orders by location and weight, optimizing vehicle usage and reducing freight costs instantly.

Zero-Blind-Spot Tracking

Don't wonder where your materials are. AutoGate integrates FastTag, SIM Tracking, and GPS APIs into a single dashboard. You get a "Single Version of Truth" for every shipment, regardless of which transporter is used.

Precision TAT Analytics

Every minute a truck sits idle in your plant is a hidden cost. AutoGate automatically benchmarks and flags any vehicle exceeding its allotted time, providing categorized delay remarks (Loading, Documentation, Security) to help fix operational leaks.

Automated Compliance & Verification

Eliminate the risk of unauthorized or unverified vehicles entering your facility. Our Vehicle Verification Module validates documents and vehicle numbers against the assigned order before the gate even opens.

Dispute Resolution & Audit Trails

Stop the "Transporter vs. Plant" blame game. With a comprehensive audit trail—including user attribution and timestamps—you have the evidence needed for transparent billing and dispute resolution.
