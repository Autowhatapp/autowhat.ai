PROPRIETARY DECISION ARCHITECTURE

Systems of Record for Decisions

Traditional systems capture data. Autowhat AI captures decision traces—the exceptions, overrides, precedents, and cross-system context that actually run enterprises.

Rules vs Decision Traces

Rules (What Should Happen)

General policies like "use official ARR for reporting" tell agents what should happen in standard cases.

Decision Traces (What Actually Happened)

Capture the specific case: which definition was used, which policy version applied, who approved exceptions, and which precedents were cited during the execution of a workflow.

The Context Graph

Not just the model's chain-of-thought, but a living record of decision traces stitched across entities and time. Precedent becomes searchable.

  • Exception logic captured at decision time
  • Precedent from past decisions becomes searchable
  • Connects related context across silos
