Core Platform Capabilities

1. Intelligent Order Management & Load Planning

Eliminate hours of manual spreadsheet manipulation. Our system ingests your raw order data and automatically architects the most efficient dispatch plan.

  • Seamless Data Ingestion: Upload order data via Excel (.xlsx, .xls, or .csv) and let the system automatically parse and validate required fields.
  • Algorithmic Grouping: The system automatically clusters orders based on nearby geographic locations (within ~100 km).
  • Capacity Optimization: Orders are dynamically assigned to vehicles based on exact weight requirements and available truck capacities.
  • Automated Transporter Dispatch: The platform automatically selects transporters and sends comprehensive email summaries.

2. Comprehensive Fleet Tracking & Trip Lifecycle

Gain absolute real-time control over your vehicles and cargo with our live monitoring and trip management services.

  • Interactive Live Mapping: Monitor all active vehicles on a unified dashboard, powered by real-time GPS hardware feeds.
  • End-to-End Milestone Tracking: Manage the complete trip lifecycle with a structured event log.
  • Freight Bidding Module: Integrated tools for comprehensive freight rate management.
  • Secure Cloud Document Storage: Safely store and instantly retrieve critical trip sheets and PODs via Google Cloud Storage.

3. Yard Orchestration & Dispatch Floor Automation

Bridge the communication gap between interior administration and exterior dock workers to accelerate loading and unloading times.

  • Digital QR Gate Passes: Issue structurally encoded HTML5 QR-code gate passes for verifiable scanning.
  • Live Line-Side Synchronization: Track active unloading yard states in real-time to eliminate vehicle idle times.
  • Conversational AI: Leverage advanced OpenAI voice processing for rapid, hands-free operational data input.

4. Automated Inventory & Compliance Verification

Protect your operations from data discrepancies, human error, and compliance risks through automated, continuous auditing.

  • Multi-Format Ingestion Engine: Automatically normalize headers across diverse stock records and space files.
  • Evidence-Based Verification: Cross-references internal work efficiency claims against external evidence sources.
  • Risk Scoring: Automatically calculates confidence scores and determines operational risk statuses for processed data.
  • Vehicle Compliance Checks: Automatically verify critical transporter documents, tracking expiry dates.

5. Proactive Communication & Support Ticketing

Keep your drivers, distributors, and management teams perfectly aligned without relying on manual phone calls or chaotic group chats.

  • Automated WhatsApp Microservice: Deliver operational notifications directly to stakeholders via the Meta WhatsApp Business API.
  • Centralized Support Ticketing: Field staff can log issues with priority classification, chat threading, and SLA enforcement.
  • On-Demand PDF Reporting: Instantly generate professionally formatted, date-filtered PDF reports detailing KPI statistics.

Enterprise-Grade Architecture & Deployment

Our platform is engineered for high availability, security, and effortless horizontal scaling to support growing enterprise operations.

Full-Stack Framework

The system is built on a monolithic Next.js (App Router) foundation, seamlessly serving a responsive, accessible React frontend alongside serverless-style API route handlers.

Cloud-Native Orchestration

Containerized as Docker images, the platform is deployed to Google Kubernetes Engine (GKE) on GCP. Zero-downtime rolling updates and Horizontal Pod Autoscaling (HPA) sustain peak loads.

Automated CI/CD & Resilient Data Layer

Transactional data is managed by MongoDB Atlas clusters. Media is persisted in Google Cloud Storage. A GitHub Actions pipeline manages Docker builds, Trivy scans, and GKE rollouts.
